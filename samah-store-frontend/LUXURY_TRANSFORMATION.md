## üé® DESIGN PRINCIPLES TO MAINTAIN

### Do's ‚úÖ

1. **Use Generous White Space**
   - Don't crowd elements
   - Let content breathe
   - Use section-padding class

2. **Keep Curves Soft**
   - rounded-2xl minimum
   - rounded-full for buttons
   - No sharp corners

3. **Transitions Are Slow**
   - 400-600ms duration
   - Elegant easing
   - Transform + opacity combos

4. **Typography Hierarchy**
   - Serif for headlines
   - Sans for body
   - Light weights (300-400)

5. **Colors Stay Soft**
   - Blush, nude, cream
   - Charcoal, not black
   - Subtle gradients OK

### Don'ts ‚ùå

1. **No Harsh Colors**
   - Avoid pure black #000
   - Avoid neon bright colors
   - Avoid high saturation

2. **No Aggressive Motion**
   - No bounces
   - No spins
   - No quick snaps

3. **No Generic Layouts**
   - Avoid Amazon-style grids
   - Avoid boxy containers
   - Avoid cluttered sections

4. **No Decorative Excess**
   - Don't add meaningless shapes
   - Don't overuse gradients
   - Keep it minimal
